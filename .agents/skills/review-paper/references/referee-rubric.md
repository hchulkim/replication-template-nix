# Referee Rubric

## Core dimensions

- contribution and novelty
- identification and empirical strategy
- data and evidence
- writing and presentation
- journal fit

## Review checklist

1. What is the paper's main claim?
2. What is genuinely new relative to the nearest literature?
3. Is the identification credible for the stated claim?
4. Do the results support the paper's strongest statements?
5. Is the paper written clearly enough for referees and editors?
6. What journal tier actually fits this draft?

## Recommendation bands

- 90+ accept
- 80-89 minor revisions
- 65-79 major revisions
- below 65 reject
