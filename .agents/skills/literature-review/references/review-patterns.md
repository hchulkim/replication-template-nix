# Review Patterns

## Preferred outputs

- annotated bibliography
- gap memo
- contribution map
- section draft for a literature review

## Minimum fields per paper

- citation
- question
- design or data
- main finding
- direct relevance to this project

## Review checklist

1. Identify the closest three to five papers.
2. Group papers by mechanism, design, or empirical claim.
3. Note points of disagreement, not just agreement.
4. Separate what the literature establishes from what your project adds.
5. End with a concrete contribution statement.

## Useful output shape

```markdown
| Paper | Question | Design/Data | Main finding | Why it matters here |
|---|---|---|---|---|
```

Then add a short memo:

- established baseline in the literature
- missing margin
- this project's contribution
