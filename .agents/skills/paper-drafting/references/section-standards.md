# Section Standards

## Introduction

- state the question early
- explain why it matters
- preview the design
- report the main finding with units
- state the contribution relative to nearby papers

## Data and methods

- define sample, treatment, outcome, and controls
- keep notation consistent
- explain the identifying assumption in prose before equations

## Results

- lead with the main table or figure
- distinguish statistical from economic significance
- do not overclaim beyond the estimand

## Style guards

- cut filler like "interestingly" or "it is worth noting"
- avoid generic AI transitions
- keep claims anchored to tables, figures, or citations
- maintain one notation system across the manuscript
